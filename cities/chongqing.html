Chongqing

Mountain City & Hot Pot Capital

Overview

Chongqing, known as the 'Mountain City', is built on hills along the Yangtze River. This massive municipality is famous for its hot pot, stunning night views, and unique urban landscape with buildings constructed on steep slopes. As one of China's four direct-controlled municipalities, Chongqing offers a vibrant mix of traditional culture and rapid modernization.

Must-See Highlights

Top Attractions

Hongya Cave, Ciqikou Ancient Town, Yangtze River Cableway, Jiefangbei CBD, Three Gorges Museum

Local Cuisine

Chongqing Hot Pot, Spicy Noodles, Jiangtuan Fish, La Zi Ji (Spicy Chicken)

Best Time to Visit

Spring (March-May) and Autumn (September-November) with comfortable temperatures

Travel Tips

  • Wear comfortable shoes for walking on hills and stairs
  • Take the Yangtze River Cableway for amazing city views
  • Try Chongqing hot pot but be prepared for extreme spiciness
  • Visit Hongya Cave at night for spectacular illuminated views

Quick Facts

Basic Info

Population: 32.1 million

Province: Chongqing Municipality

Language: Mandarin, Southwestern Mandarin (Chongqing dialect)

Timezone: China Standard Time (UTC+8)

Getting There

Airport: Chongqing Jiangbei International Airport (CKG)

Train: High-speed rail from Chengdu, Wuhan, Shanghai (2-8 hours)

Best Transport: Monorail, metro, buses, and taxis (be prepared for hills!)
