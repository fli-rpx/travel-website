Qingdao

Where East Meets West

Overview

Qingdao is a major port city in Shandong Province, famous for its European architecture, beautiful coastline, and Tsingtao Beer. Originally a German concession, the city blends Chinese and Western influences with its red-roofed buildings, Catholic churches, and coastal scenery.

Must-See Highlights

Top Attractions

Zhanqiao Pier, Badaguan Scenic Area, Tsingtao Beer Museum, Laoshan Mountain

Local Cuisine

Seafood, Tsingtao Beer, Braised Abalone, Clams in Black Bean Sauce

Best Time to Visit

Spring (April-June) and Autumn (September-October) with mild temperatures and clear skies

Local Cuisine

Must-Try Dishes

Fresh Seafood: Clams in Black Bean Sauce, Braised Abalone, Steamed Fish

Tsingtao Beer: World-famous beer brewed locally since 1903

Seafood Hotpot: Fresh seafood cooked in flavorful broth

Best Food Streets

Beer Street: Traditional beer halls and seafood restaurants

Badaguan Food Street: International cuisine and local specialties

Zhanqiao Pier: Fresh seafood markets and restaurants

Transportation

Getting There

By Air: Qingdao Jiaodong International Airport (TAO) with domestic and international flights

By Train: High-speed rail from Jinan, Yantai, Weihai (2-4 hours)

By Car: Well-connected highway system from major cities

Getting Around

Metro: Efficient metro system covering major attractions

Bus: Extensive bus network throughout the city

Taxi: Readily available and affordable

Ferry: Ferries to nearby islands and coastal areas

Suggested Itinerary

Day 1: Historical Qingdao

Morning: Visit Zhanqiao Pier and the iconic Huilan Pavilion

Afternoon: Explore Badaguan Scenic Area with its European architecture

Evening: Enjoy fresh seafood at Beer Street

Day 2: Beer Culture

Morning: Tour the Tsingtao Beer Museum and learn about brewing history

Afternoon: Visit the Catholic Church and Protestant Church

Evening: Sample local craft beers at waterfront bars

Day 3: Natural Beauty

Morning: Take a cable car up Laoshan Mountain for panoramic views

Afternoon: Visit the beautiful beaches and coastal parks

Evening: Watch the sunset from the Olympic Sailing Center
